Services

Flexible consulting, from focused projects to interim leadership.

Every engagement is scoped to the development stage, internal capabilities, program risks, and near-term objectives of the client. Some sponsors need a department built. Some need one problem solved.

01

Interim & Fractional Clinical Operations Leadership

Head, Executive Director, or VP-level support. Portfolio oversight, board-level reporting, organizational design, hiring plans, and team mentoring.

02

Clinical Program Strategy & Execution

FIH and proof-of-concept planning, protocol operationalization, country and site strategy, critical-path timelines, and rescue planning for programs that have stalled.

03

Clinical Operations Function Building

Infrastructure design, SOPs and process development, governance models, escalation pathways, KPI and KQI frameworks, and startup-to-scale planning.

04

CRO & Vendor Strategy

Selection, RFP strategy, bid analysis and negotiation, outsourcing models, governance committees, performance management, and CRO transition planning.

05

Site Selection, Feasibility & Recruitment

Investigator and institution research, competitive enrollment analysis, site prioritization, enrollment forecasting, activation planning, and referral-network analysis.

06

Quality, Compliance & Inspection Readiness

Sponsor oversight assessments, GCP risk reviews, TMF quality, CAPA support, for-cause investigations, data-integrity risk identification, and audit response planning.

07

Due Diligence & Program Assessment

Clinical program health checks, operational due diligence for M&A and investment, contract exposure review, and timeline and budget credibility assessments.
